About The Faculty

The Faculty of Information Technology at Misr University for Science and Technology (MUST) is a leading academic institution dedicated to shaping future professionals in the fields of Computer Science, Information Systems, and Artificial Intelligence. The faculty offers modern, industry-aligned programs that combine theoretical foundations with hands-on practical experience. With a strong focus on innovation, problem-solving, and emerging technologies, the faculty equips students with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in today’s fast-growing tech industry. State-of-the-art labs, expert academic staff, and collaborations with local and international organizations provide students with valuable training opportunities and a solid foundation for a successful career path.
