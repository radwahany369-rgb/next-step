Overview of the Artificial Intelligence Department

The Artificial Intelligence Department at the Faculty of Information Technology, Misr University for Science and Technology, is one of the modern and rapidly developing departments that began admitting its first cohort in 2020. The department aims to prepare qualified graduates capable of meeting the demands of the digital revolution and the global job market in artificial intelligence and data-related fields. The program focuses on teaching the latest technologies such as machine learning, data analysis, data mining, cloud computing, intelligent systems, and computer vision, in addition to strengthening students’ programming abilities and algorithmic thinking. It provides an integrated learning environment that combines theoretical foundations with practical applications through hands-on projects, enabling students to develop innovative solutions in various domains. Graduates of the department have wide career opportunities in roles such as AI Engineer, Machine Learning Engineer, Data Scientist, Data Engineer, Intelligent Systems Developer, Computer Vision Engineer, and Robotics Developer, which are among the most in-demand careers worldwide today. This department is an ideal choice for students interested in building intelligent applications, developing data-driven solutions, or creating systems capable of understanding and interacting with their environment. It also provides a strong foundation for innovative student projects such as smart robots and intelligent service systems.
